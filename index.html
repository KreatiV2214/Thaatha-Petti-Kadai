About Thaatha Petti Kadai

Thaatha Petti Kadai (Grandpa's Box Shop) is more than just a snack store - it's a tribute to our heritage. We bring you the authentic taste of traditional Indian snacks, made with the same love and care that our grandfathers put into every batch.

From protein-rich peanut barfi to wholesome protein balls, each product is crafted using time-tested recipes and the finest natural ingredients. We believe that healthy eating shouldn't mean compromising on taste, and our snacks are a testament to that philosophy.

Every bite takes you on a journey back to simpler times, to the warmth of grandpa's kitchen, where tradition meets health in perfect harmony.
